# Play Store Screenshot Spec

Play requires **at least 2** screenshots per form factor you submit. Recommended: 4–8 phone screenshots, plus 7" + 10" tablet sets if you want to be eligible for tablet feature placement.

## Sizes

| Form factor | Min/max edge | Aspect ratio | Format |
|---|---|---|---|
| Phone | 320 – 3840 px on the longest side | Between 16:9 and 9:16 | PNG or JPEG, 24-bit, no alpha |
| 7-inch tablet | 320 – 3840 px on the longest side | Between 16:9 and 9:16 | Same |
| 10-inch tablet | 1080 – 7680 px on the longest side | Between 16:9 and 9:16 | Same |

Recommended phone size: **1080 × 1920** (9:16) or **1080 × 2400**.

## Capture script

The same screenshots used for App Store submission generally work — just
re-export at 1080 × 1920 for Play. If you're using a Pixel 7 / 8 emulator
or a real Pixel device, the native screenshot dimensions are already valid.

## Suggested screen flow (8 frames)

1. **Hero / cover** — Soul Codex logo, tagline "Your complete soul blueprint"
2. **Onboarding** — Birth details form, mid-fill
3. **Soul Blueprint** — The synthesized archetype card (cream card on dark)
4. **Daily Guidance** — Today's reading
5. **Soul Guide chat** — A sample question + response
6. **Compatibility** — Two-person synastry result
7. **Tracker** — Congruence chart over time
8. **Birth chart poster** — The downloadable natal chart

## Caption banners (optional)

If you want banner captions across each screenshot, use:
- "Your complete soul blueprint"
- "Built from 31+ systems"
- "Daily guidance, written for you"
- "Ask your AI Soul Guide anything"
- "Compare any two people"
- "Track your alignment over time"
- "Your natal chart, ready to download"

Keep them under 7 words and in the cream/bronze palette to match the app.
