## 2. The Five Compatibility Dimensions

Your engine should score five areas. Keep it simple and human.

### Identity Alignment

Do their archetypes move in the same direction?

Example
Architect + Strategist → high alignment
Architect + Pathfinder → friction but creative

This tells users whether two people **build together or pull apart**.

---

### Emotional Rhythm

Compare emotional processing.

Example
Water-dominant + Fire-dominant

Result
Fire reacts quickly
Water processes deeply

Outcome
Possible misunderstanding unless both slow down.

---

### Stress Collision

What happens when both people are under pressure?

Example
Air stress + Air stress

Result
Both overthink. No one acts.

Example
Air stress + Fire stress

Result
One analyzes, one explodes.

The engine explains these collisions.

---

### Moral Compass

This is huge for long-term compatibility.

Example
If both reject dishonesty → strong trust base.

If one tolerates chaos and the other demands order → tension.

Values decide whether relationships **survive conflict**.

---

### Decision Dynamics

Compare decision styles.

Examples

Calm logic + calm logic
→ slow but stable decisions

Gut instinct + calm logic
→ debate between intuition and analysis

Willpower + assess
→ leadership struggle
